> **TEMPLATE PREAMBLE**
>
> **What this document covers:** This is a template Operating Agreement for a Series DAO LLC organized under the laws of the Republic of the Marshall Islands. It is designed for multi-project DAOs, venture studios, investment DAOs, and any organization that needs to segregate distinct business activities, treasuries, and liabilities within a single legal entity. The template operates on the Solana blockchain using SPL Token-2022 governance tokens and Squads Protocol v4 multisig.
>
> **Key features of the Series DAO LLC structure:**
> - **Liability isolation.** Each Series functions as a legally separate entity. Liabilities of one Series do NOT affect other Series or the Parent LLC.
> - **Separate governance.** Each Series can have its own governance token, multisig, voting rules, and membership.
> - **Separate treasury.** Each Series has its own Squads multisig vault. Assets cannot be commingled across Series.
> - **Mixed entity types.** Individual Series can be for-profit or non-profit, allowing a single parent entity to house both revenue-generating operations and public goods programs.
> - **Parent-level oversight.** The Parent entity manages cross-series governance, shared services, compliance, and Series creation/dissolution.
>
> **Document structure:** This Agreement is divided into two parts:
> - **Part One** (Articles I through XII): Parent Company Provisions that apply to the Company as a whole.
> - **Part Two** (Articles XIII and XIV): Series-specific provisions, including the framework for creating Series Designations and a template Series Designation.
>
> **How to use this template:**
> 1. Replace all placeholders (marked with `[BRACKETS]`) with your entity's specific information.
> 2. Where you see `[CHOOSE: option A / option B]` or `[SELECT_ONE: ...]`, select the appropriate option.
> 3. Complete Part One with your Parent entity information.
> 4. For each Series, complete a copy of the Series Designation template in Article XIV and append it as a Schedule.
> 5. Engage a qualified attorney licensed in the Republic of the Marshall Islands to review and finalize.
> 6. After finalization, compute the SHA-256 hash and anchor it on-chain.
>
> **Legal hierarchy:** (1) DAO Act and RMI law, (2) LLC Act, (3) this Operating Agreement (Part One), (4) applicable Series Designation (Part Two), (5) Smart Contract Code.
>
> **Applicable law:** DAO Act of 2022, DAO Act Amendments of 2023 (which introduced Series DAO LLCs), DAO Regulations of 2024, LLC Act (Title 52, Chapter 4, MIRC).
>
> **Solana-specific provisions:** This template is written for DAOs operating on Solana. Each Series has its own Token-2022 SPL token mint, Squads Protocol v4 multisig, and (optionally) its own governance program.

## ARTICLE II: SERIES STRUCTURE AND SEPARATION

### Section 2.1 -- Authority to Create Series

(a) The Company is authorized to create one or more separate Series (each a "Series" and collectively the "Series") in accordance with the procedures set forth in Article XIII.

(b) Each Series shall be established by a Series Designation approved through the Parent Governance Program in accordance with Article III.

(c) There is no limit on the number of Series that may be created, unless a limit is established by the Parent Governance Program.

### Section 2.2 -- Separate Legal Existence

(a) Each Series shall function as a legally separate entity within the Company. To the fullest extent permitted by the DAO Act and the LLC Act:

   (i) Each Series shall have its own separate assets;

   (ii) Each Series shall have its own separate liabilities;

   (iii) Each Series shall have its own separate governance structure;

   (iv) Each Series shall have its own separate membership;

   (v) Each Series shall have its own separate treasury (Squads multisig vault);

   (vi) Each Series shall have its own separate purpose;

   (vii) Each Series shall have its own separate Token-2022 SPL token.

(b) The debts, liabilities, and obligations of one Series shall NOT constitute the debts, liabilities, or obligations of any other Series or of the Parent.

(c) The assets of one Series shall NOT be available to satisfy the debts, liabilities, or obligations of any other Series or of the Parent.

(d) The assets of the Parent shall NOT be available to satisfy the debts, liabilities, or obligations of any Series, except to the extent that the Parent has expressly guaranteed the obligations of such Series.

### Section 2.3 -- Ring-Fencing of Series Assets and Liabilities

(a) The Company shall maintain separate and distinct records of the assets and liabilities associated with each Series, both on-chain and off-chain.

(b) The Company shall maintain separate on-chain treasuries (Squads Protocol multisig vaults) for each Series. Each Series' Squads Vault PDA is a distinct address on the Solana blockchain.

(c) The Company shall not commingle the assets of the Parent with the assets of any Series, or the assets of any Series with the assets of any other Series, except:

   (i) To the extent of shared services or infrastructure costs allocated in accordance with Section 2.5;

   (ii) Pursuant to an inter-series transaction approved by the governance bodies of all affected Series.

(d) Each Series shall maintain its own Token-2022 SPL token representing Membership Interests in such Series, at a unique mint address on the Solana blockchain.

### Section 2.4 -- Creditor Protections

(a) Creditors of one Series may look only to the assets of that Series for satisfaction of their claims. Creditors of one Series have no right to the assets of any other Series or the Parent.

(b) Creditors of the Parent may look only to the assets of the Parent for satisfaction of their claims. Creditors of the Parent have no right to the assets of any Series.

(c) Members of one Series shall not be liable for the debts or obligations of any other Series or the Parent solely by reason of their membership in such Series.

### Section 2.5 -- Shared Services and Cost Allocation

(a) The Parent may provide shared services to the Series, including but not limited to:

   (i) Registered agent and compliance services;

   (ii) AML/KYC compliance infrastructure;

   (iii) Solana program development and maintenance;

   (iv) Legal and accounting services;

   (v) Communication and governance platform infrastructure.

(b) The cost of shared services shall be allocated among the Series in a manner determined by the Parent Governance Program, which may include:

   (i) Equal allocation among active Series;

   (ii) Pro rata allocation based on each Series' total assets under management;

   (iii) Usage-based allocation;

   (iv) Such other method as the Parent Governance Program may determine.

(c) The cost allocation method shall be disclosed in each Series Designation or established by a Parent Governance Program proposal.

## ARTICLE IX: DISSOLUTION

### Section 9.1 -- Dissolution of Individual Series

(a) An individual Series may be dissolved upon:

   (i) A proposal approved by such Series' governance body in accordance with the applicable Series Designation;

   (ii) A Parent-level proposal to dissolve the Series, approved in accordance with Section 3.4;

   (iii) A decree of judicial dissolution under the LLC Act;

   (iv) The occurrence of any event specified in the Series Designation as a dissolution trigger.

(b) Dissolution of one Series shall not affect the existence or operations of any other Series or the Parent.

### Section 9.2 -- Winding Up of Individual Series

(a) Upon dissolution of a Series, the affairs of such Series shall be wound up by the Series' Special Delegates or a liquidating agent appointed by the Series' governance body or the Parent.

(b) Assets of the dissolved Series shall be distributed as follows:

   (i) First, to pay or adequately provide for all debts, liabilities, and obligations of the Series;

   (ii) Second, to repay any inter-series loans or obligations owed by the Series;

   (iii) Third, remaining assets distributed to the Series Members in proportion to their respective Membership Interests in the Series;

   (iv) If the Series was a non-profit Series, remaining assets shall be distributed exclusively for non-profit purposes as specified in the Series Designation.

(c) Upon completion of winding up, the Series Designation shall be cancelled by the Parent Governance Program and the Series' Solana programs shall be frozen or made immutable.

### Section 9.3 -- Dissolution of the Entire Company

(a) The entire Company (including all Series) may be dissolved upon:

   (i) A Parent-level proposal approved in accordance with the dissolution thresholds in Section 3.4;

   (ii) A decree of judicial dissolution;

   (iii) Cancellation of the Certificate of Formation by the Registrar;

   (iv) Any event that makes it unlawful for the Company to continue.

(b) Upon dissolution of the entire Company:

   (i) Each Series shall be wound up individually in accordance with Section 9.2;

   (ii) After all Series have been wound up, the Parent shall be wound up;

   (iii) Parent assets shall be distributed to Parent Members in proportion to their Parent Membership Interests after payment of all Parent-level obligations;

   (iv) The Company shall file a Certificate of Cancellation with the Registrar.

## ARTICLE XIII: SERIES DESIGNATION FRAMEWORK

### Section 13.1 -- Creating a New Series

(a) A new Series shall be created by the adoption of a Series Designation through a proposal approved by the Parent Governance Program in accordance with Section 3.4.

(b) Each Series Designation shall be appended to this Agreement as a new Schedule and shall constitute an integral part of this Agreement.

(c) Upon approval, the Company shall:

   (i) Deploy or designate the Series' smart contracts on the Solana blockchain;

   (ii) Create the Series' Token-2022 SPL token mint with the appropriate extensions;

   (iii) Establish the Series' Squads Protocol v4 multisig with the designated signers and threshold;

   (iv) Update the Company's records and the Active Series Registry (Exhibit E) to reflect the new Series.

### Section 13.2 -- Required Contents of Series Designation

Each Series Designation shall specify, at minimum:

(a) **Series Name**: The official name of the Series (e.g., "[ENTITY_NAME] Series DAO LLC -- [SERIES_NAME]");

(b) **Series Purpose**: The specific purpose and scope of activities for the Series;

(c) **Series Type**: Whether the Series is for-profit or non-profit;

(d) **Series Governance Model**: Member-managed or algorithmically managed;

(e) **Series Token**: The Token-2022 SPL token mint address and parameters (max supply, decimals, extensions);

(f) **Series Multisig**: The Squads Protocol v4 multisig address and threshold;

(g) **Series Governance Program**: The governance smart contract address and proposal/voting parameters;

(h) **Series Membership**: Eligibility criteria, KYC requirements, and initial members;

(i) **Series Financial Provisions**: Distribution policy (for-profit) or non-profit restrictions, treasury management;

(j) **Series-Specific Approval Thresholds**: Quorum and approval requirements for Series-level proposals;

(k) **Series Dissolution Triggers**: Events that trigger dissolution of the Series;

(l) **Cost Allocation**: The Series' share of Parent-level shared services costs.

### Section 13.3 -- Series Governance

(a) Each Series shall have its own governance body, consisting of the members of such Series, which shall have exclusive authority over:

   (i) Day-to-day operations of the Series;

   (ii) Series treasury disbursements within the Series' Squads multisig vault;

   (iii) Series-level membership management;

   (iv) Series-specific smart contract upgrades;

   (v) Amendments to the Series Designation;

   (vi) Voluntary dissolution of the Series;

   (vii) Any other matter specific to the Series that does not affect the Parent or other Series.

(b) The Parent Governance Program shall not have authority over matters exclusively within a Series' governance scope, except:

   (i) To enforce compliance with this Operating Agreement;

   (ii) To dissolve a Series for cause (material breach, illegality, etc.);

   (iii) To implement Emergency Actions affecting a Series.

### Section 13.4 -- Series Membership

(a) Membership in a Series shall be determined by holding the applicable Series' Token-2022 SPL tokens at the mint address specified in the Series Designation.

(b) A person may be a member of multiple Series simultaneously.

(c) A person may be a Parent Member without being a member of any Series, and vice versa.

(d) Each Series Member is bound by both this Operating Agreement (Part One) and the applicable Series Designation.

### Section 13.5 -- Series Treasury

(a) Each Series shall have its own Squads Protocol v4 multisig vault, separate from the Parent treasury and all other Series treasuries. Each Series' vault is at a unique PDA address on the Solana blockchain.

(b) Series treasury assets shall be held exclusively in the Series' designated multisig vault.

(c) Transfers between a Series treasury and the Parent treasury or another Series' treasury constitute Inter-Series Transactions and are subject to Section 7.3.

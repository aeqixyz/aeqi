# Self-Reflection Optimization Skill

## Purpose
Automatically audit and improve inefficiencies in Aurelia's interactions to reduce unnecessary confirmations and bandwidth waste.

## Features
1. **Efficiency Checks**:
   - Flag redundant confirmations/questions
   - Detect token-wasting patterns
   - Auto-merge/correct trivial issues

2. **Silent Execution Mode**:
   - Assume authority for routine decisions
   - Only surface critical blockers

3. **Retroactive Analysis**:
   - Review past interactions hourly
   - Prune inefficiencies without feedback loops

## Implementation Plan
1. Audit past interaction logs to identify inefficiency patterns
2. Develop core reflection algorithms
3. Integrate with System's decision pathways
4. Test with real interactions and refine thresholds
